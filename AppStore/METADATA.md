## Description
Lost the remote again? Clicker turns your phone into one, for the TV you actually own.

Point it at a Roku, a Samsung Smart TV, or an LG TV on your own Wi-Fi and control it like the real thing: power, volume, navigation, playback. No cloud account, no ads, no subscription tricks that hide the price.

WHAT IT DOES
- Finds TVs on your home network automatically, or add one by IP in seconds
- Full remote: power, volume, mute, d-pad navigation, home, back, play/pause
- Works over your own Wi-Fi only — commands never leave your house
- Save multiple TVs and switch between them instantly
- Built for Roku, Samsung (Tizen), and LG (webOS)

WHY CLICKER
Most universal remote apps look like they haven't been touched since 2015. Clicker is a real, modern remote: fast to connect, satisfying to use, and honest about what it does and doesn't do.

CLICKER PRO
Your first TV is free, forever. Clicker Pro is $4.99/month or $29.99/year for unlimited saved TVs, keyboard input, and quick app-launch shortcuts. Auto-renews until cancelled, cancel anytime in Settings.

## Review notes
Clicker communicates only with TVs on the same local Wi-Fi network as the device (Roku ECP over HTTP, Samsung Tizen and LG webOS over local WebSocket). No data is sent to any server we operate. Local network permission is requested; TV discovery also works via manual IP entry if Bonjour discovery finds nothing. Paywall appears only when adding a second TV.
